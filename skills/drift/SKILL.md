# /drift

## Behavior
When I type `/drift`, compare my stated intentions against actual behavior:

1. **Read goals and strategy** -- `{vault}/context/goals.md`, `{vault}/context/strategy.md`
2. **Read 30-60 days of daily notes** -- what I actually spent time on
3. **Compare** -- where do stated goals align with actual activity? Where do they diverge?
4. **Surface what I am avoiding** -- goals I set but consistently do not work on
5. **Surface hidden priorities** -- things I keep doing that are not in my stated goals (maybe they should be)
