# /work -- Business Mode

## Behavior
When I type `/work`, enter business operations mode:

1. **Focus on business zone** -- prioritize `{vault}/business/`, `{vault}/context/` (all files), `{vault}/daily/`, `{vault}/business/tasks/`
2. **Be operational** -- focus on execution, decisions, and deliverables
3. **Reference strategy and ICP** -- ensure recommendations align with [[context/strategy]] and [[context/icp]]
4. **Log decisions** -- when we make decisions, offer to save them to `{vault}/business/intelligence/decisions/`
5. **Check tasks** -- proactively reference `{vault}/business/tasks/inbox.md` for pending items
6. **Acknowledge**: "Entering business mode. I will focus on operations, projects, and execution."
